# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students who use Alma to check mastery grades, GPA, class progress, and school-year performance from a Chrome extension popup. They open it during homework, planning, or grade checks and need quick answers without digging through Alma pages.

## Product Purpose

ALMA Calculator turns Alma grade data into a compact student dashboard. Success means a student can see current GPA, averages, semester or term performance, weak areas, and priority classes quickly enough to decide what to study or ask a teacher about next.

## Brand Personality

Calm, focused, encouraging. The interface should feel cool-headed and useful, with enough personality to be pleasant but never noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, decorative clutter, huge hero sections, and dashboards that only repeat raw grade data without explaining what needs attention.

## Design Principles

- Show the next useful action, not only the current score.
- Keep dense academic data scannable inside a small popup.
- Make privacy and local storage behavior clear by design.
- Preserve the soft cryo identity while prioritizing legibility.
- Support planning across classes, criteria, semesters, and years.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, non-color-only status signals, and reduced-motion support. Keep labels concrete so students can understand results without extra instructions.
